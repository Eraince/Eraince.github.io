Rachel Handler

is an American actress, singer and motivational speaker who is best known for playing Chunks in the 2016 horror movie Smothered. Rachel holds a degree in Vocal Performance from the Westminster Choir College. Six years ago she lost her left leg in a car accident and was fortunately able to salvage her knee, which increases her ability to maneuver her leg. Rachel now walks with prosthesis and has become an advocate for actors with disabilities. There are times when she choses to expose her prosthetic leg so that strangers may see it and ask her about it, showing that there can be opportunity in adversity. She used to think of her prosthesis as ‘the torture device’ because it was extremely painful, but now she says there’s no better feeling than looking down at her own two feet and taking a step. Either a step towards the fridge, or a step onto the stage.

The Hack

To overcome difficulties encountered in her daily rosutines, Rachel has found a couple of hacks to help her move around. One of those is by using a wheeled desk chair to do the laundry.
