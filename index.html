Jobs at Toastmasters

An important element of Toastmasters is everyone partipates. In every meeting, every one has a role. Either actively as a speaker or evaluator or the Toastmaster of the Day, or as attentive listeners who give feedback.

  • Toastmaster of The Day

    The Toastmaster is a meeting’s director and host. You’re responsible for ensuring all of the meeting’s players know their parts and hit their marks. As the Toastmaster, you’ll introduce each speaker. If a speaker will not write his or her own introduction, you will write it.
